=== CHANGE MANAGEMENT PROPOSAL ===

Cyber corp needs to deploy a new firewall

=TECHNICAL CHANGE MANAGEMENT

> A request is sent to the to the IT team, for the deployment of a new firewall, the request contains the reason for the change, the scope etc

> Approval

> Testing the change in a controlled environment (sandbox)

> The deployment of the firewall

> Feedback from the users/stakeholders

> A rollback / backup, if something goes wrong
